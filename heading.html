HEADING TAGS

Definition:
In HTML, heading tags (h1 to h6) are used to define headings in a webpage. h1 is the largest and most important heading, while h6 is the smallest. They help structure content and improve readability as well as SEO.
Examples:

SAI RAM (h1)

SAI RAM (h2)

SAI RAM (h3)

SAI RAM (h4)

SAI RAM (h5)
SAI RAM (h6)

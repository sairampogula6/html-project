FORMATTING TAGS

Definition:
Formatting tags in HTML are used to change the appearance of text, such as making it bold, italic, underlined, or highlighted. They help in emphasizing important parts of content and improving readability. Examples include bold, italic, underline, superscript, subscript, and mark.

Examples:

  1. SAI RAM (bold(b))

  2. SAI RAM (strong)

  3. SAI RAM (italic(i))

  4. SAI RAM (underline(u))

  5. x2 (superscript(sup))

  6. H2O (subscript(sub))

  7. SAI RAM (mark)

  8. SAI RAM (strickout(del))

  9. SAI RAM (underline(ins))
